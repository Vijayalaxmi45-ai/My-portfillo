RESUME FILE FOR PORTFOLIO
=========================

Copy your resume PDF into this folder with the exact filename:

  MyResume.pdf

Steps:
1. Locate your resume on your laptop (e.g. in your softskill folder).
2. Copy the file and paste it here, OR copy it to:
   c:\All-Projects\my_Portfillo\public\resume\
3. Make sure the file is named exactly: MyResume.pdf

After that, the "Resume" link in the navigation and "Download Resume" button on the home page will open/download your resume.
